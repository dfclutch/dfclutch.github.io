Education
At Ohio State, I am studying Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence. I am interested in the theoretical side of computer science, rather than the software-engineering focused side. Algorithms and math are generally where I feel most comfortable. Some of my favorite course include:

  • CSE2321 - Discrete Structures: My first introduction to Graphs, Proofs, Set Theory, etc.
  • CSE3321 - Finite Automata and Formal Languages: One of the highest level theory classes in the OSU undergrad sequence - a class on computability theory and its associated mathematics
  • CSE5526 - Neural Networks: Taught by DeLiang Wang, director of the Ohio State Perception and Neurodynamics lab, Neural Networks is probably the most interesting and engaging class I've taken at OSU

While I enjoy theoretical computer science mostly, I feel I am a mature programmer and software designer. My education outside of class - through internships and personal study - has made me an excellent writer of clean, readable, maintainable code. The most valuable thing I took away from my Summer 2019 internship with John Deere was a practical sense of how and why writing beautiful code is necessary.
