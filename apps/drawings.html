Laurel Lake

Laurel lake is an old quarry pond in Pine Grove Furnace state park in southern Pennsylvania. The park contains the halfway point of the Appalachian Trail and the general store that hosts the ice cream challenge thru-hikers partake in. The drawing features a rock outcropping on Piney Mountain above the lake called Pole Steeple.
