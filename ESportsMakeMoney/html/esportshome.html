If you have children between the ages of about 5 and 20, you probably know something about "ESports." Whether it's Fortnite, League of Legends, Call of Duty, Super Smash Bros, any other entry on the seemingly endless list of video games, kids are playing them - and watching others play them - more than ever before. With increasing popularity has come increasing possibility to profit from being a skilled video game player. As crazy as this sounds, people are making real money (Think professional athelete or musician money) off playing video games. This website purposes to provide information about the fiscal side of ESports, in an attempt to prove their value as an interest or hobby for kids.
