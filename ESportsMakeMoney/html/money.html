Fans
There are approximately 130 Million active ESports fans worldwide (Simic, 2017). This number is only increasing as game development improves and tournament interest increases. Esports are globally viewed, as streams of competitions and tournaments are often widely available in most countries.
Source: Garland, 2016. <http://www.seattleweekly.com/news/competative-gaming-hits-the-big-time-in-seattle/>

Above is a break down of where the money made by ESports athletes comes from. Like more traditional sports, most of the profit comes from advertising through sponsors. Sponsors

Continued Growth
Sponsorship money makes up around 40% of the $900 Million net worth of Esports. However, as ESports becomes more widely viewed and the monetary potential increases, it is expected that media rights for Esports events will significantly increase the worth of Esports in the upcoming years. (Perez, 2018)
